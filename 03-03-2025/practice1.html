Royal Enfield

Royal Enfield is an iconic motorcycle brand that has been in existence for over a century. It is known for its classic and rugged motorcycles that have a cult following worldwide.

History

Royal Enfield was originally a British motorcycle company founded in 1901. It became famous for its strong and durable motorcycles, especially during wartime. The company was later bought by Eicher Motors in 1990, and it has since revived as one of the most respected motorcycle brands in India and internationally.

Design Features

Royal Enfield motorcycles are known for their vintage design, which combines classic looks with modern technology. The design features include:

  • Chrome accents for a retro look
  • Classic round headlamp and twin-pod speedometer
  • Thick leather seats for comfort
  • Large fuel tanks for long-distance rides
